FAQ

Questions before you request an estimate.

How fast will someone respond?

Most inquiries receive a callback within one business day. Estimate timing depends on project size, photos, location, and schedule availability.

Do you handle small repair jobs?

Yes. Drywall patches, paint touch-ups, fixture changes, trim repairs, and prep work can often be handled as smaller projects.

What areas do you serve?

The service area includes the Bronx, Riverdale, Pelham Bay, Morris Park, Throgs Neck, Yonkers, Mount Vernon, and nearby lower Westchester neighborhoods.

Can homeowners supply their own materials?

Yes, depending on the material and project scope. The estimate can clarify what is owner-supplied versus contractor-supplied.
